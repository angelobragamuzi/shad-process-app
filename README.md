# ShadProcess - Legal Case Management

## Overview

This application is a **Legal Case Management System** designed to help legal professionals efficiently manage, monitor, and analyze judicial processes. The app centralizes case information, documents, deadlines, and insights in a single platform, improving organization, productivity, and decision-making.

It provides structured data entry through forms, intelligent dashboards, calendar-based tracking, and AI-powered legal assistance integrated directly with the case database.

---

## Key Features

### Case Registration & Management

* Structured forms for registering legal cases
* Full case lifecycle management
* Registration of all involved parties (plaintiff, defendant, attorneys, etc.)
* Centralized storage of case-related information

### Document Management

* Upload and associate documents with each case
* Organized access to procedural documents
* Secure storage linked directly to the respective process

### Case Calendar & Tracking

* Calendar view for monitoring case deadlines and events
* Visual follow-up of procedural timelines
* Improved control over important dates and obligations

### Dashboards & Analytics

* Interactive dashboards for case data visualization
* Overview of process metrics and indicators
* Quick insights to support strategic and operational decisions

### AI-Powered Legal Assistant

* Integrated AI chatbot connected to the case database
* Context-aware legal assistance based on registered cases
* Support for procedural analysis, summaries, and guidance

### Reports (In Development)

* Generation of customized reports based on registered data
* User-defined report requests
* Export of reports to **PDF format**

---

## Technology Overview

* **Frontend:** Mobile-first application developed with Flutter
* **Architecture:** Modular and scalable
* **Data Management:** Local persistence with Drift Database, structured for cases and documents
* **AI Integration:** Contextual chatbot powered by Gemini AI Agent with direct database access
